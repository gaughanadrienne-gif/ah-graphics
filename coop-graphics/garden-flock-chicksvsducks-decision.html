Which Bird Is Right for Your Situation?

A practical verdict framework for Santa Cruz County backyards

Start with Chickens if...
  • Your yard is small or the run is tight
  • Mud management is not feasible for your space
  • You want familiar, mild-flavored eggs
  • Neighbors are very close
  • You prefer a simpler daily routine
  • You are new to poultry and want to learn first
  • Slug pressure in your garden is low
Start with Ducks if...
  • Slug and snail damage is a serious garden problem
  • You want consistent winter egg production
  • You have drainage to manage water mess
  • You bake regularly and want richer eggs
  • Your yard gets very wet in winter and stays wet
  • You want calmer, less flighty birds
  • Noise is a concern (female ducks vs. hens)
Consider Both if...
  • You have room for two separate areas or species
  • You want slug control and familiar eggs together
  • You are ready for more complex daily management
  • Your garden has distinct bed zones to rotate birds through
  • You have experience with one species already
