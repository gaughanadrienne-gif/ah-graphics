Recognizing Heat Stress in Your Flock

A quick-reference guide to signs by severity level

Early Stage

Monitor and cool proactively

  • Panting, beak open
  • Wings slightly out
  • Seeking shade
  • Eating less
  • Drinking more

Moderate Stage

Act immediately

  • Labored breathing
  • Pale or faded comb
  • Lethargy, won't move
  • Unsteady gait
  • Wings hanging low

Emergency

Life-threatening, intervene now

  • Unresponsive, won't stand
  • Seizure-like trembling
  • Gasping, not panting
  • Blue or purple comb

Source: UC ANR Publication 8457, Poultry Heat Stress; Merck Veterinary Manual, Poultry Health
