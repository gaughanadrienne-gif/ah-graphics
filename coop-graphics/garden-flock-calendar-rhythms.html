Year-Round Flock Management Rhythms

Regular cycles that underpin seasonal care

Daily
  • Collect eggs; check nest boxes
  • Verify all birds present and alert at morning release
  • Refill fresh water
  • Lock up at dusk (or verify automatic door closed)
Weekly
  • Refresh roost bar bedding; spot-clean floor
  • Scrub and disinfect waterers
  • Check feed supply; inspect rodent-proof storage
  • Walk perimeter for predator probing
Monthly
  • Inspect each bird: keel condition, eyes, vent, feathers
  • Check for external parasites (mites, lice)
  • Monitor feed consumption vs. baseline
  • Inspect hardware cloth and coop for damage
Twice Yearly
  • Full coop deep-clean (Oct and late April/May)
  • Treat for red mites if detected
  • Audit predator-proofing completely
  • Review flock health records and plan any replacements
Annually
  • Order spring chicks if adding to flock (January)
  • Deep-audit run drainage before rainy season
  • Review feed costs and storage setup
  • Assess flock composition and productivity
