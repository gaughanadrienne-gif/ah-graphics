Backyard Flock First-Aid Kit Checklist

Stock these before you need them -- California backyard keepers

Wound Care

  • ✓Sterile saline wound wash
  • ✓Vetericyn or chlorhexidine spray
  • ✓Gauze pads (3x3 and 4x4 inch)
  • ✓Rolled gauze
  • ✓Vet wrap (self-adhesive)
  • ✓Styptic powder
  • ✓Antibiotic ointment (plain, no pramoxine)

Tools

  • ✓Blunt-tip scissors
  • ✓Tweezers or hemostats
  • ✓Digital thermometer (dedicated)
  • ✓Headlamp

Supportive Care

  • ✓Electrolyte/vitamin powder (Sav-A-Chick)
  • ✓Epsom salt
  • ✓Disposable nitrile gloves
  • ✓Old bath towels (2-3)

Isolation Setup

  • ✓Dog crate or large tote (hospital pen)
  • ✓Small waterer and feeder for hospital pen
  • ✓Notebook for observation notes
