Which Path Is Right for You?

Quick decision guide for Santa Cruz County flock keepers

Choose Hatchery Chicks If...

  • You want a specific breed (Barred Rock, Australorp, Easter Egger)
  • You have children who will raise birds hands-on
  • You want a full 2 to 4 years of productive laying ahead
  • You enjoy the brooding process and have the setup
  • You plan to start from scratch with no existing flock
  • Chick season is currently active at your local feed store

Choose Rescue Hens If...

  • You want eggs within weeks, not months
  • You do not want to brood chicks for six weeks
  • You are adding birds to an existing adult flock
  • Giving retired hens a good home is personally meaningful
  • You live where roosters are prohibited and want no surprises
  • Good birds are currently available at the local shelter or rescue
