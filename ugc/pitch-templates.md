# UGC Pitch Kit — Templates, Targets, and Rate Card

Everything needed to go from portfolio → first paid brief. Personalize the bracketed
parts; keep everything else short. Brands read pitches on their phones.

---

## Rate card (internal reference)

Public page says "from $175." Your private floor while building the first 5 paid pieces
is **$150** — take it if the brand is a name worth having in the portfolio. After 5 paid
videos, floor moves to $250; after 10, re-quote everything at $300–350.

| Item | Rate |
|------|------|
| Single UGC video (30–60s, organic use) | $175 (floor $150) |
| 3-video bundle | $450 |
| Monthly partner (4 videos/mo) | from $600/mo |
| Paid ad usage rights | +30% per 90 days |
| Whitelisting / Spark Ads | +$100 per video per month |
| Extra hooks | +$50 each |
| Raw footage | +$75 |
| Rush (72h) | +25% |

**Never included for free:** ad usage, whitelisting, exclusivity, raw files, perpetual
rights. "Full buyout" or "in perpetuity" in a contract = quote 3–4× the video rate.

## Template 1 — Cold email to a brand

**Subject options (test all three):**
- Garden UGC from a certified consultant's real garden
- [Product name] content idea — shot in a working Zone 9b garden
- Short-form video for [Brand] from an actual gardener

> Hi [Name],
>
> I'm Adrienne — certified garden consultant, and I grow food year-round in a coastal
> California garden (plus a backyard flock). I create short-form UGC for garden and
> homestead brands: demos, unboxings, how-tos, and results videos, shot on location
> in my garden.
>
> For [Brand], I'd pitch something like: **[one specific concept for THEIR product —
> e.g., "a 45-second 'why chicken wire fails' story that ends on your hardware cloth"]**.
>
> Portfolio: **ambitiousharvest.com/work-with-brands**
>
> Single videos start at $175 with 7-day turnaround, and I plan seasonal content around
> real planting windows — fall-garden content needs to shoot in August, not October.
>
> Worth a quick chat this month?
>
> Adrienne Gaughan
> Ambitious Harvest · Santa Cruz, CA

**The one rule:** the bracketed concept line is the pitch. A specific idea for *their*
product outperforms any credential. Spend your 5 minutes of research there.

## Target list — 25 pitches for month one

Aim for **25 personalized pitches in July**; expect 3–6 replies and 1–3 paid briefs.
Track in a simple sheet: brand / contact / date sent / concept pitched / status.

**Category A — warm (send week 1):**
1–4. Stellar panel brands and any company whose product you've formally reviewed
5. Brands you already buy from repeatedly (your order history is a credential)

**Category B — perfect-fit niches (weeks 1–2):** backyard poultry & coop gear (auto
doors, feeders, predator-proofing), seed companies with CA/coastal ranges, soil &
amendment brands, drip irrigation / watering.

**Category C — volume (weeks 2–4):** hand tool brands, raised bed & greenhouse kits,
garden organization/apparel, pest control (humane/organic).

**Where to find the contact:** LinkedIn → search "[brand] marketing" or "influencer";
email patterns first.last@brand.com; or the DM route via Template 2. Smaller brands
(under ~50 employees) reply far more often — prioritize them over Scotts-sized companies.

**Platforms (set up profiles week 1, then let briefs come to you):**
Billo, Insense, JoinBrands, Trend. Use the same 5 portfolio videos; platform briefs
pay less ($60–150) but fill gaps between direct deals and add logos to the portfolio.

## Negotiation quick answers

- **"What's your rate?"** → "Single videos are $175 for organic use; the 3-video bundle
  at $450 is what most brands start with. If you want to run them as ads, usage is +30%
  per 90 days." Always answer with numbers immediately — hesitation reads as amateur.
- **"Can you do it for product only?"** → "I include the product in every paid project,
  but I don't work on gifted-only collabs — the garden's a working business. Happy to
  start with a single video if budget's tight." (Exception: you may take ONE gifted deal
  from a dream-logo brand for the portfolio. One.)
- **"We need full rights."** → "Perpetual buyout is quoted separately — most brands find
  the 90-day renewable usage more cost-effective. Want both numbers?"
- **"Can you post it on your channels too?"** → That's a different product (influencer
  posting, not UGC). "My packages are for content you post on your channels. Posting to
  mine is quoted separately based on the campaign." Don't give away distribution for free.
- **Exclusivity** (they ask you not to work with competitors) → time-boxed and paid:
  +50% for 90 days within their exact category, never a handshake agreement.
